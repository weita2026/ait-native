LicenseRef-AIT-Commercial

This identifier names the non-public commercial license surface used by
release-facing `ait` license documents for components that are not granted
under the repository root `LICENSE`.

Scope of this reference:

- commercial self-hosted rights for components that are otherwise marked
  `AGPL-3.0-only OR LicenseRef-AIT-Commercial`
- commercial-only product surfaces such as `ait-web` and enterprise
  extensions when release-facing docs mark them with this identifier
- package targets, deployment bundles, or managed offers that require a
  separate written commercial agreement

This file is a command-layer reference text only.

It is not a complete commercial agreement, customer subscription contract,
hosted-service terms, patent license instrument, or enterprise order form.
Those terms must be supplied through separate counsel-reviewed commercial
documents before customer-facing use.

Until a separate commercial agreement is executed, this identifier does not
grant public open-source rights beyond the licenses explicitly stated
elsewhere in the repository.
